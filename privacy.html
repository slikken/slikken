Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 12/07/2023


Introduction


Welcome to SensiFlori's Privacy Policy page. In the age of digital transparency, we firmly believe that every user should be well-informed about the ways in which their data is handled. This policy outlines our commitment to safeguarding your personal information. Here, you will find detailed explanations on what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.


Collection of Personal Information


SensiFlori, your trusted service provider, values the relationship we have with you. The personal information we collect aids in understanding your unique preferences, ensuring that we offer a tailored experience every time you interact with our platform. By doing this, we aim to consistently elevate our services and make them more aligned with your needs.


Purpose of Collection


Your personal information is more than just data to us—it's a means to create a more personalized and efficient experience for you. By collecting this data, we can: Deliver content that is most relevant to your preferences. Innovate and refine our services based on feedback and usage patterns. Keep you informed about updates, offers, and other information that might interest you.


Opt-Out Instructions


Your comfort and trust are paramount. If at any point you decide that you no longer wish to receive certain communications from us, or if you have any reservations about the data we hold, you can contact us directly at privacy@SensiFlori.com. We respect your choices and are always available to cater to your privacy preferences.


Sharing and Disclosure:


Your trust is invaluable to us, and we pledge never to betray it. While we do not engage in the business of selling, trading, or renting out your personal data for marketing gains, there are instances where sharing is necessary for operational purposes. In such cases, we only collaborate with verified service providers and partners who are bound by stringent confidentiality agreements.


Compliance with Laws


At Zipox Media, our practices are grounded in a deep respect for the law. We are committed to adhering to all pertinent data protection legislations and regulations, ensuring that your personal information remains secure, confidential, and handled with utmost care.


Cookies and Analytics:


In our pursuit to offer a seamless digital experience, we utilize cookies and advanced analytical tools. While these technologies help us understand website traffic patterns and enhance user experience, the information collected is non-personal. This means that your individual identity remains anonymous.


Third-Party Links:


While our platform might contain external links, we want to emphasize that we have no control over third-party websites. Their content, actions, and privacy practices are outside our purview. As a precaution, we recommend that users acquaint themselves with the privacy statements of any third-party site they visit.


Children's Privacy:


SensiFlori’s services are exclusively designed for adults. It is not our intention to solicit or collect data from individuals under the age of 18. If we inadvertently gather such information, immediate steps will be taken to delete it from our records.


Changes to Privacy Policy:


As we evolve, so might our Privacy Policy. While changes will be made judiciously, we will ensure that significant modifications are communicated to our users either through the platform or direct email notifications.


Contact Information


Your feedback and concerns matter. For any clarifications, suggestions, or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the data we hold, please reach out to us at privacy@SensiFlori.com. Remember, by using SensiFlori's services, you signify your acceptance of this policy. Regularly visiting this page will keep you updated on any changes. We appreciate your trust in SensiFlori, where your privacy is always at the forefront.
